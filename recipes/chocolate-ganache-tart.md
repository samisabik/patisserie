# Chocolate Ganache for Tart

## Ingredients

- 50 g milk
- 120 g single cream (30-35% fat)
- 120 g good-quality dark chocolate (Valrhona Caraïbe 66% or Cacao Barry Mexique 66%)
- 12 g butter
- 2 small eggs

## Recette

1. Preheat the oven to 150 °C.
2. Chop the chocolate into a mixing bowl and melt it gently over a bain-marie or in the microwave (1 minute at 500 W, stir, then repeat).
3. In a saucepan, bring the milk, butter and cream to the boil. Let cool for about 30 seconds.
4. Pour this mixture over the chocolate. Mix gently, stirring from the centre to create an emulsion, without making bubbles.
5. Lightly beat the eggs, then add them to the mixture.
6. Pour into the pre-baked (blind-baked) tart shell, bake for 15-20 minutes, then turn off the oven to let it finish cooking gently.
7. Check the cooking by tapping lightly: the ganache should remain slightly wobbly.
8. Leave to cool at room temperature, then refrigerate for about 2 hours before serving.

## Notes

Unlike a true ganache, this recipe is closer to a chocolate flan. The cream stays soft and melting after baking.

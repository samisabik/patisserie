# Chocolate and Pecan Brownie

## Ingredients

- 2 whole eggs
- 120 g butter
- 100 g + 40 g dark chocolate (70%)
- 60 g milk chocolate
- 80 g blond (light brown) sugar
- 40 g flour
- 125 g pecans
- 2 pinches of fleur de sel

## Recette

1. Preheat your oven to 160 °C. Butter and flour your tin, or line it with a well-fitted sheet of baking paper.
2. In a hot pan, toast the pecans for a few moments. Set aside. Meanwhile, break your chocolate into pieces (as a reminder, 100 g dark chocolate and 60 g milk chocolate) and melt with the butter over a bain-marie or in the microwave (30 seconds at 900 W, then stir and reheat in further 10-second bursts if needed).
3. Then, in a mixing bowl, combine the eggs and sugar using a spatula or a whisk. Add the melted chocolate and butter mixture and mix well. Add the flour and the fleur de sel. Mix again.
4. Add the very lightly crushed toasted pecans and the remaining 40 g of dark chocolate.
5. Pour into the tin and bake for 20 minutes, no more, if you want your brownie to stay gooey in the centre. Leave to cool slightly before enjoying. Serve it, why not, with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for extra indulgence!

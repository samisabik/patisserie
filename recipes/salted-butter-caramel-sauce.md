# Salted Butter Caramel Sauce

## Ingredients

- 180 g sugar
- 110 g salted butter
- 200 ml double cream (40% fat)
- 4 pinches of fleur de sel

## Recette

1. Put the butter, the fleur de sel, and the sugar in a thick-bottomed saucepan. Add a splash of water to just moisten the sugar.
2. Heat over a medium-high heat, stirring with a wooden spoon, until everything has melted and begins to colour. Adjust the heat up or down as needed depending on what you see happening in the pan.
3. Keep stirring so the bottom does not burn as the caramel forms.
4. Off the heat, once the caramel has a lovely brown colour, add the double cream. Pour it in gradually, stirring between each addition to avoid spattering.
5. Return to the heat and bring back to the boil to dissolve any remaining sugar crystals.
6. Pour the hot caramel into a clean jam jar and leave to cool before refrigerating.

## Notes

The proportions are adjusted so the sauce stays soft and spreadable at room temperature, yet becomes deliciously runny and coating once warmed. Once you have dipped a finger in, it is impossible not to become completely hooked!

# Quick Shortcrust Pastry

## Ingredients

- 300 g flour
- 150 g butter, diced and softened
- 0.5 tsp salt
- 3 tbsp sugar (if making a sweet pastry)
- 80 ml lukewarm water or milk

## Recette

1. Mix the flour and salt in a dish (and the sugar if using).
2. Add the butter, then work it into the flour by kneading quickly and lightly with your fingertips. You should obtain a kind of coarse semolina within 2 to 3 minutes.
3. Quickly work in the milk or water. You need very little for the dough to come together and form a ball. Milk or water? It is down to taste.
4. To roll out without trouble, roll the dough out on baking paper and bake it as it is on the tray.

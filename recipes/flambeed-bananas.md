# Flambéed Bananas

## Ingredients

- 2 ripe bananas
- 1 sachet vanilla sugar (about 7.5 g)
- 1 small glass rum (about 40 ml), or Grand Marnier
- 2 small knobs of butter

## Recette

1. Heat a frying pan, melt the butter in it and add the bananas cut lengthways. Brown on each side.
2. Sprinkle with the vanilla sugar, pour in the rum and flambé: once the flames have died down, serve.
